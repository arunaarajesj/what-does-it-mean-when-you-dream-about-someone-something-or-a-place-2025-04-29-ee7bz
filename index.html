Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are dream interpretations?

Our interpretations are based on established psychological research, cultural symbolism, and personal context. While accuracy varies, we maintain a 85% satisfaction rate among users who confirm our interpretations align with their experiences.

What makes DreamGuide different from other interpretation services?

We combine traditional dream analysis with modern AI technology, offering personalized interpretations that consider your unique circumstances and emotional patterns.

How can I improve my dream recall?

Keep a dream journal by your bed, maintain a consistent sleep schedule, and practice mindfulness before bed. Our app includes tools specifically designed to enhance dream memory.

Do you offer professional consultation?

Yes, we provide access to certified dream analysts for one-on-one consultations through our premium service tier.

How often should I record my dreams?

We recommend daily dream journaling for best results, but even recording dreams 2-3 times per week can provide valuable insights into your subconscious patterns.
